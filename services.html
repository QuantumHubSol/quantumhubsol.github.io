Placement Services

Placement Stages

  • Sourcing candidates:This involves searching for identifying suitable candidates through various methods such as job boards, Social Media, Personal networking and Seeking from our Personal Candidate Data Base.
  • Screening and shortlisting: candidates: Our Recruiters reach out the Candidates by Cold Calling and match with job Description and relevant candidates resumes and profiles of the candidates and shortlists the most suitable ones.
  • Interviewing candidates: We conduct initial interviews to assess the candidate's skills and suitability for the role.
  • Salary Negotiation: Our Team will also help to negotiate salaries, benefits, and other employment terms between the client and the candidate.
